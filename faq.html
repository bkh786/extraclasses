Parent FAQ

Below are answers to common questions about Extra Classes. We want to ensure all parent doubts are removed.

1. How are Extra Classes different from other tuition classes?

Extra Classes provides small batch tuition (maximum 5 students) so that every child gets personal attention. We also conduct monthly tests and share progress reports with parents, which helps in tracking the student’s improvement.

2. What is the batch size?

We keep a maximum of 5 students per batch to ensure personal attention and better understanding for every student.

3. Do you provide all subjects?

Yes.

  • Class 1–5: All subjects
  • Class 6–8: Maths, Science, English, Social Studies, or All Subjects
Have a quick question? Chat on WhatsApp
4. Do you provide online or offline classes?

We provide both:

  • Online Classes (Small Batch)
  • Offline Classes (Available if student and teacher are nearby)
5. Do you provide demo classes?

Yes, we provide 2 free demo classes so that parents and students can experience our teaching before joining.

6. How long is each class?

Each class is 1 hour 30 minutes:

  • First 15 minutes – Homework & doubt solving
  • Next 75 minutes – Concept teaching and practice
Still have doubts? Ping us here
7. Do you help with school homework?

Yes, we help students with homework, school concepts, and exam preparation so that they stay up to date with school studies.

8. How do you track student progress?

We conduct:

  • Monthly Test
  • Performance Evaluation
  • Monthly Progress Report
  • Teacher Feedback
  • Parent Feedback

Parents are regularly updated about the student’s performance.

9. What if we are not satisfied with the teacher?

If there is any issue, parents can inform us and we will arrange a replacement teacher or batch change.

Always here to help
10. How are the fees paid?

Fees are paid monthly in advance. Details will be shared after demo classes.

11. How do we communicate with teachers?

All communication will be done through Extra Classes platform/WhatsApp, so that progress, feedback, and support can be properly managed.

12. What is the benefit of small batch tuition?

In small batches:

  • Students get personal attention
  • Students can ask doubts freely
  • Teachers can focus on weak areas
  • Learning becomes more effective
  • Students become more confident
13. Which boards do you cover?

We cover:

  • CBSE
  • ICSE
  • State Boards
14. How do we enroll?

You can enroll by:

  • Filling the registration form on the website, or
  • Messaging us on WhatsApp to book demo classes.
